Lentil Wrap

Description

Lentil wrap with cucumbers, bell peppers, and cherry tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole wheat lavash bread
  • 350-400g cooked red lentils (with garlic powder, chili powder, turmeric, and black pepper)
  • 75-100g cucumber, diced
  • 75-100g bell pepper, diced
  • 75-100g cherry tomatoes, halved or quartered

Steps

  1. Place lavash bread on a wide flat surface (cutting board works well)
  2. Spread lentils evenly on the lower 4/5 of the lavash bread
  3. Toss the chopped veggies in a bowl until evenly mixed
  4. Add the chopped veggies on top of the lentils
  5. Roll the lavash bread so the veggies are in the middle and the bread overlaps itself
  6. Slice in half to form two even wraps
