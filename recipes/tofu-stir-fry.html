Tofu Stir Fry

Description

A quick and easy stir fry with tofu and vegetables

Ingredients

  • 2 packages of tofu (~1 lb each)
  • 1 bag of stir fry veggies (~1 lb)
  • 1 package of mushrooms (optional)
  • 1 bell pepper (optional)
  • 1 onion (optional)
  • 1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1/8 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/8 tsp smoked paprika

Steps

  1. Press the tofu for at least 20 minutes
  2. Wash and slice the mushrooms (if using)
  3. Chop the bell pepper and onion (if using)
  4. Cut the tofu into 1" cubes and set aside
  5. Fry the mushrooms (if using) on medium high heat in a nonstick pan until they begin to carmelize
  6. Add the onion and bell pepper (if using), cook until translucent
  7. Add the stir fry veggies and cook until water is mostly gone
  8. Add the tofu and spices, then cook for a few more minutes, stirring frequently
