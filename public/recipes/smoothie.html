Blueberry Smoothie

Description

A delicious blueberry smoothie that is healthy, delicious, and satiating.

Ingredients

  • 150g fresh banana
  • 100g frozen blueberries
  • 100g frozen mango
  • 75g rolled oats
  • 50g raw almonds
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 324g water

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to high speed blender
  2. Blend thoroughly until smooth
